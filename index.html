About

I am a Full Professor in the Mathematical Engineering Institute at the Faculty of Engineering at the Universidad de Valparaíso (UV) and a member of the research centre CIMFAV.

In late 2006, I obtained my PhD in Statistics from the University Paris-Sud Orsay under the supervision of Marc Lavielle (INRIA, École Polytechnique) and Jean-Louis Foulley (INRA, Jouy-en-Josas).

My research interests include Mixed Models, EM-type algorithms, REML estimation, MCMC methods and applications in biometrics.
